## Authority hierarchy

When documents conflict, resolve them in this order:

1. applicable law, binding contracts, insurer requirements, and sanctioning-body rules;
2. founder-approved decision records and venture governance;
3. approved competition, finance, operating, and risk policies;
4. approved product and architecture specifications;
5. working notes, assumptions, and drafts.

## Required review cadence

- Founder decision register: weekly until pilot registration opens.
- Financial model and cash forecast: monthly, then weekly from registration opening through season close.
- Risk register and emergency plan: before every season and after every material incident.
- Competition rules: before registration opens; freeze at the published rules date except for safety or legal changes.
- Product roadmap: at each release gate.
- Sponsor inventory: before sale, after contracting, and after fulfillment.
